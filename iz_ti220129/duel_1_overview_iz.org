* Terminology

** Random move

The player chooses at random from one of the 6 tactics with equal probability:

#+begin_src sclang
tactics[player] = tactic = (0..5).choose;
#+end_src

** Optimal move (optimal tactic)

The player chooses the tactic which has the greatest chances for success based on the tactic chosen by the opponent and the values of the matrix of the game matrix.
